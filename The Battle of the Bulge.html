The Battle of the Bulge

The Battle of the Bulge, also known as Hitler's final gamble in Western Europe was a ferocious Counter-attack that
took place in the snowy forests of Bastogne. The attack was spearheaded by three Panzer enormous armies. The Allies quickly found themselves unprepared and ill-equiped. As the battle raged on across Ardennes, it was left to a few U.S Infantry Divisions to hold the Alied lines.
